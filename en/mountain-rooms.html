Rooms with Internal View

In a welcoming atmosphere you could enjoy the fresh breeze coming from the mountains that every night blows giving a great feeling of freshness.

Every accommodation is provided with:

  • Mini Fridge
  • Bathroom with a shower, a travel hairdryer and towels.
  • TV
  • Free High-speed Wi-Fi access

Important information:

The check-in is available from 02.00 pm and the check-out limit is 10.00 am.
Once you get to the town’s main street (Via Benamati), you will find the parking on your right after 500 meters. Once you have checked-in you will be provided with the remote for the main gate of our facility.
Breakfast is served between 08:00 and 10:00. You will find a rich smorgasbord, salty and sweet, whit delicious homemade cakes. The costumers with no breakfast included can access it at the price of € 10 per person.
