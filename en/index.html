ABOUT US

The Hotel Vittoria is located in the heart of Maderno village. Our facility is comfortable and quiet: it is situated in a residential area surrounded by olive trees, it disposes of large and bright spaces in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Our priority is to meet all of your needs with careful attention and a special care for details.

SERVICES

The Swimming Pool

The hotel disposes of a private swimming pool for our guests, with two different depths: the first is 1.50 m deep, suitable for adults, and the second is 1 m deep, suitable for children.

Breakfast

Breakfast is served between 08:00 and 10:00. You will find a rich smorgasbord, salty and sweet, with delicious homemade cakes.

The Garden

In the garden you can find sun umbrellas, lawn chairs and tables where you can comfortably eat lunch/dinner in the open air.

The Parking

The parking is inside the facility. When you check-in we will provide you with a door opener so you will be able to move whenever you want.

Pets

Our facility is pet-friendly. Pets must be on a leash inside the hotel and they cannot be let unattended in your room.

The Bar

We offer you a bar service between 12:00 and 18:30. We have a simple but tasty menu.
